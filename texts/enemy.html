This island has a moderate presense of goverment militarized police (Gendarmerie) and Horizon Islands Defence Force (HIDF).

Gendarmerie is the primary peace force on the island of Moddergat, where they holdand guard several key buildings, one of which we suspect to be the place for intelligence operations. Gendarmerie is armed primarily with pistol caliber carbines and pistols, wear body armor, and use civilian trucks and vans for transport.

HIDF is mainly armed with M4/M16 rifles, FN MAG/MINIMI machineguns, lightly armored trucks and HMMWVs. No heavy wehicle The coast guard base has some static defenses on its perimeter, and RHIB boats are known to be as their main way of water transport. HIDF Air Force primarily uses UH-1 transport and gunship helicopters.
