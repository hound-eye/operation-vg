The conflict at Horizon Islands is heading for re-igniting, after seemingly lasting armistice between the various faction and organizations, biggest of which being the government of Horizon Island Republic, and a breakaway separatist formation Tanoan Liberation Army (TLA).

Recently, the government police (Gendarmerie) and military (HIDF) conducted a series of joint counter-insurgency operations on republic's peripheral territories. A group of intelligence officers may came across critical information about local weapon caches and TLA collaborators.

TLA high command was notified of this situation by the local criminal organization. They offered financial support and further assistance, should your team of TLA commandos successfully obtain and hand over this intel.
